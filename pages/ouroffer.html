Rubber conveyor

RUBBER CONVEYOR

They are mostly used to transport large quantities of loose material, but also for transporting individual articles, packets, products etc. They are used in almost every industry branch, especially mining, construction industry, agriculture, petrochemical industry, wood industry etc. They are made up of the so called “core” and the protective rubber layer. The standard series is intended for general conditions of use of workloads ranging from: 16, 21, 26, 32, 40 and 52kg/cm of the belt’s width.

In accordance with these workloads we offer belts with a core made up of 2,3, 4, or 4 polyester-polyamide (EP) fabrics of high tensile strength. Due to the excellent transversal flexibility they have the ability to adjust to local deformations, operational blows and vertical curves.

The external rubber layer protects the belt from environmental influences, wear and tear, etc. even in the presence of oil, acid and heat in transported materials. It meets the essential local and foreign standards. Delivered in standard widths: 300 ; 400 ; 450 ; 500 ; 600 ; 650 ; 800 ; 1000 ; 1200 ; 1400 ; 1600 mm or wider based on the needs of the client.
Connection of belts

CONNECTION OF BELTS

Thanks to the expertise and experience of our staff, we are able to professionally and efficiently cater to the needs of our clients in servicing belts as well. Whether it is connecting belts using the cold or hot vulcanization process or repairing the belt, we guarantee absolute reliability and quality regardless of where the intervention may be performed. We use technologically advanced and high quality materials in the process of repair and adhesion, even in unfavourable environmental conditions we achieve good and long-term results. The installation process of the belts is preceded by correct dimension calculations, workload amount and the characteristics of the transporter. The dimensions of the belt depend on the amount and characteristics of the transported material and the length and delevelling of the transport. Transition distances, the radius of curvature and the tension tempo are determined based on the elastic elongation of 1.3.
Adhesion

ADHESION

Plastic cement – adhesive for bonding PVC belts For cold vulcanization bonding of PVC belts we offer Plastic Cement (PC: in accordance with Loba authorization Nr – Nv 84.12.41.3.36 and hardener UT – R20 in accordance with Loba authorization NW 12.22.67.1.25). Repair paste For quick and efficient filling of cracks, cuts and defects on the rubber layers, we offer CONREPA adhesive which are placed onto the treated surface using a spatula. In accordance with Loba standards for use in low ventilated spaces. Delivered in cans of 0.7 kg. PRIMER For preventive covering of metal surfaces we recommend primer PR 200, PR 300 or PR 400. Allows for a higher solidity of adhesive joints and prevents oxidation. On average, 100 g of primer is enough for and area of 1 m². Applied with a brush. Material for hot vulcanization The choice of material for hot vulcanization of conveyor belts depends on the composition of the rubber layer of the belt. Depending on the sensitivity of the material, the space where it is stored it should be ventilated, protected from light with a temperature range between +5 and +25 C. If the material is stored according to regulation it can last for 6 months from the production date.
